FAQ

How do I respond to a notification?

Long-press (or swipe down on) the notification to reveal Complete and Skip buttons. Or tap the notification to open the app and respond there.

How do notifications work during my sleep schedule?

Tap the moon icon in the top-right corner to set your sleep window. Reminders are automatically paused during those hours.

What does the premium subscription include?

Free users can track up to 3 habits. Premium ($1.99/year) unlocks unlimited habits and custom notification sounds.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone, find Moneo, and tap Cancel.

Is my data backed up?

Habit data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, your data is permanently removed. No cloud backup is available at this time.
