Moneo Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 1, 2026

Moneo ("the App") is a micro-habit tracking app developed by Joshua Taylor ("we", "us"). Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App handles and how.

Data We Do Not Collect

Moneo does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. Specifically:

  • We do not require account creation or login.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not access your location, contacts, camera, microphone, or health data.

Data Stored on Your Device

All habit data (names, schedules, streaks, and completion history) is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

If you delete the App, all locally stored data is permanently removed.

Notifications

Moneo uses local notifications to deliver habit reminders. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device and do not involve any external push notification service. No notification data is sent to us or any third party.

Subscriptions

Moneo offers an optional premium subscription managed entirely through Apple's App Store and processed by RevenueCat, Inc. We do not receive or store your payment information. Apple and RevenueCat handle all billing. You can review their privacy policies at:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

Moneo is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.
